## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU, UK, Switzerland Users)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under GDPR:

#### 7.1.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).

#### 7.1.2 Right to Rectification
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update most information directly in your account settings.

#### 7.1.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data when:
- The data is no longer necessary for the purposes collected
- You withdraw consent (where consent was the legal basis)
- You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds
- The data has been unlawfully processed
- Deletion is required by legal obligation

Note: We may retain certain data for legal obligations, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes.

#### 7.1.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request restriction of processing when:
- You contest the accuracy of the data (during verification)
- Processing is unlawful but you prefer restriction to deletion
- We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims
- You have objected to processing (pending verification of overriding grounds)

#### 7.1.5 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your personal data in a portable format (JSON or CSV) and transmit it to another service where technically feasible.

#### 7.1.6 Right to Object
You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes. We will cease processing unless we have compelling legitimate grounds that override your interests.

#### 7.1.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

#### 7.1.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (supervisory authority) if you believe we have violated your rights.

**EU Supervisory Authorities:** https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en  
**UK Information Commissioner's Office:** https://ico.org.uk/  
**Swiss Federal Data Protection Authority:** https://www.edoeb.admin.ch/

#### 7.1.9 Automated Decision-Making
We do not make decisions based solely on automated processing (including profiling) that produce legal effects or similarly significantly affect you. Our content recommendations and personalization features do not fall under this category, but you can opt-out of personalized recommendations in your settings.

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

#### 7.2.1 Right to Know
You have the right to request disclosure of:

#### 7.2.4 Right to Non-Discrimination

You may designate an authorized agent to make CCPA requests on your behalf. The agent must provide proof of authorization, and we may require you to verify your identity directly with us.

## 9. Data Security

### 9.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Encryption:**
- **In Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security)
- **At Rest:** Sensitive data (passwords, OAuth tokens, payment info) is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Database Encryption:** PostgreSQL databases use encryption at rest
- **Backup Encryption:** All backups are encrypted

**Access Controls:**
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Required for all staff with access to production systems
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Principle of least privilege - staff have minimum necessary access
- **Audit Logs:** All access to sensitive data is logged and monitored
- **Background Checks:** All employees with data access undergo background checks

**Password Security:**
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt (never stored in plain text)
- Minimum password strength requirements enforced
- Password reset via secure, time-limited tokens
- Account lockout after multiple failed login attempts

**Security Monitoring:**
- 24/7 automated monitoring for security threats
- Intrusion detection systems (IDS)
- Vulnerability scanning and penetration testing
- Security incident response plan

**Network Security:**
- Firewalls and network segmentation
- DDoS protection
- Rate limiting and throttling
- IP allowlisting for administrative access

**Application Security:**
- Regular security audits and code reviews
- Dependency scanning for vulnerabilities
- OWASP Top 10 compliance
- CSRF protection on all state-changing requests
- SQL injection prevention (parameterized queries)
- XSS protection (input sanitization, output encoding)

**Third-Party Security:**
- All third-party service providers undergo security assessments
- Data processing agreements (DPAs) in place
- SOC 2 Type II compliance required for critical providers
- Regular security audits of third-party integrations

### 9.2 Security Limitations

**No Guarantee:**
No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**Your Responsibility:**
- Keep your password secure and confidential
- Do not share your account credentials
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (when available)
- Log out of shared or public devices
- Keep your device and software updated
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 9.3 Data Breach Notification

**In the Event of a Breach:**
- We will investigate and contain the breach within 24 hours
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
- We will notify supervisory authorities as required by law
- We will provide details of the breach, affected data, and remediation steps
- We will offer assistance (credit monitoring, identity theft protection) if appropriate

**How We Will Notify You:**
- Email to your registered email address
- Prominent notice on our website and mobile apps
- In-app notification
- Media notification (for large-scale breaches)

**What You Should Do:**
- Change your password immediately
- Monitor your account for suspicious activity
- Enable two-factor authentication (if not already enabled)
- Review your payment and billing information
- Contact us with questions or concerns

## 10. Data Retention

### 10.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

**User Account Data:**
- **Active accounts:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive accounts:** Retained for 2 years after last login, then deleted
- **Deleted accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days, except for legal/security exceptions

**Submission and Content Data:**
- **Public submissions:** Retained indefinitely (or until you delete them)
- **Deleted submissions:** Permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Comments:** Retained indefinitely (or until you delete them)
- **Voting history:** Retained indefinitely (for content ranking integrity)

**Payment and Subscription Data:**
- **Active subscriptions:** Retained as long as subscription is active
- **Canceled subscriptions:** Retained for 7 years (tax and accounting requirements)
- **Transaction records:** Retained for 7 years (legal and financial compliance)
- **Stripe data:** Subject to Stripe's retention policies (typically 7 years)

**Analytics and Usage Data:**
- **Analytics data:** Retained for 13 months (rolling window)
- **Aggregate statistics:** Retained indefinitely (anonymized)
- **Session data:** Retained for 90 days

**Log Data:**
- **Application logs:** Retained for 90 days
- **Security logs:** Retained for 1 year
- **Audit logs:** Retained for 7 years (compliance requirements)
- **Error logs:** Retained for 90 days

**Communication Data:**
- **Support inquiries:** Retained for 3 years
- **Email correspondence:** Retained for 3 years
- **Marketing emails:** Retained until you opt-out

**Authentication Data:**
- **OAuth tokens:** Retained until you disconnect the service
- **Session tokens:** Expire after 30 days or logout
- **Password reset tokens:** Expire after 1 hour

### 10.2 Reasons for Retention

We retain data for the following purposes:
- **Service delivery:** Provide and improve the Service
